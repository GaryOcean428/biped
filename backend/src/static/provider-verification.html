7-Step Verification Process

Our comprehensive verification process ensures only qualified, trustworthy professionals join our platform.

1

Identity Verification

Required Documents: Government-issued photo ID (driver's license or passport) + proof of residential address

Process: Real-time ID verification using advanced document authentication technology

Checks: Document authenticity, photo matching, address validation

2

Business Registration Verification

Required: Valid Australian Business Number (ABN) and business registration

Process: Direct verification with Australian Business Register (ABR)

Checks: Active business status, GST registration, business structure validation

3

Professional License Verification

Required: Trade-specific licenses and certifications (where applicable)

Process: Direct verification with relevant state licensing bodies

Checks: License validity, expiry dates, disciplinary history, specialization areas

4

Insurance Verification

Required: Public liability insurance (minimum $2M), workers' compensation (if applicable)

Process: Direct verification with insurance providers

Checks: Coverage amounts, policy validity, premium payment status

5

Background Checks

Scope: Criminal history, bankruptcy records, court judgments

Process: National police check and financial background screening

Checks: Relevant criminal history, financial stability, legal compliance

6

Reference and Work History Verification

Required: 3 recent customer references, previous employer verification

Process: Direct contact with references, work quality assessment

Checks: Work quality, professionalism, reliability, customer satisfaction

7

Skills and Portfolio Assessment

Required: Portfolio of recent work, skills demonstration

Process: Expert review of work samples, practical skills assessment

Checks: Technical competency, work quality standards, safety compliance
