About Biped Platform

Our Mission

Biped is revolutionizing the trades industry by connecting skilled professionals with customers through an AI-powered digital marketplace. We believe that everyone deserves access to quality tradesman services, and every skilled professional deserves fair opportunities to grow their business.

What We Do

Our platform combines cutting-edge artificial intelligence with industry expertise to create seamless connections between:

  • Customers who need professional services for their homes and businesses
  • Service Providers who offer skilled trades and professional services

Our Technology

Smart Matching

Our AI algorithms analyze project requirements, provider capabilities, location, and availability to create perfect matches for every job.

Computer Vision

Advanced computer vision technology tracks project progress, ensures quality standards, and provides automated progress reporting.

Secure Payments

Escrow-based payment system protects both parties, with automated release upon project milestones and quality verification.

Autonomous Operations

Automated workflows handle scheduling, invoicing, progress tracking, and quality assurance, reducing administrative overhead.
