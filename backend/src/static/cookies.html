1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website. They enable the website to recognize your device and store some information about your preferences or past actions. Cookies help us provide you with a better experience and allow certain features of our platform to function properly.

2. How We Use Cookies

Braden Pty Ltd uses cookies on the Biped Platform to:

  • Enable essential website functionality and security
  • Remember your login status and preferences
  • Analyze website performance and user behavior
  • Provide personalized content and recommendations
  • Deliver targeted advertising and marketing communications
  • Improve our AI matching algorithms and platform features

5. Cookie Consent and Management

5.1 Your Consent

When you first visit our website, you will see a cookie banner asking for your consent to use non-essential cookies. You have the following options:

  • Accept All: Consent to all cookie categories
  • Reject Optional: Only allow essential cookies
  • Cookie Settings: Choose specific cookie categories

5.2 Managing Your Preferences

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by:

  • Using the cookie preference center above
  • Clicking the "Cookie Settings" link in our website footer
  • Configuring your browser settings to block or delete cookies
  • Using browser extensions that manage cookie consent

6. Browser Cookie Controls

6.1 Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies and website data
  • Edge: Settings > Site permissions > Cookies and site data

6.2 Mobile Devices

  • iOS Safari: Settings > Safari > Privacy & Security
  • Android Chrome: Settings > Site Settings > Cookies
  • App-based browsing: Check individual app privacy settings

7. Impact of Disabling Cookies

Important Notice

Disabling certain cookies may impact your experience on our platform. Some features may not work properly or may be unavailable.

7.1 Essential Cookies

If essential cookies are disabled, you may experience:

  • Inability to log in or maintain login status
  • Security vulnerabilities and protection gaps
  • Website functionality errors
  • Payment processing failures

7.2 Performance Cookies

Disabling performance cookies means:

  • We cannot improve website performance based on usage data
  • Less effective troubleshooting of technical issues
  • Reduced ability to optimize user experience

7.3 Marketing Cookies

Without marketing cookies:

  • You may see less relevant advertisements
  • Remarketing campaigns will not function properly
  • Social media integration may be limited
